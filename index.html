Vegan Buffalo Chicken Bake

This vegan and dairy-free buffalo chicken bake is a delicious and healthy alternative to the classic dish. Perfect for those following a vegan or dairy-free diet!

Ingredients

  • 3 cups shredded chicken substitute (e.g., jackfruit, soy curls)
  • 2 12 oz bags frozen cauliflower rice
  • 1/4 cup coconut cream (dairy-free)
  • 1 sweet onion, diced
  • 2 green onions, sliced
  • 1 bottle dairy-free buffalo sauce
  • 1 bottle dairy-free ranch dressing
  • 1 jalapeño, diced
  • 2 tbsp garlic powder
  • 1 cup vegan cheese (e.g., Daiya, Follow Your Heart)
  • 1 red pepper, diced
  • 1 avocado, sliced for garnish

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine shredded chicken substitute, cauliflower rice, coconut cream, diced onion, and buffalo sauce. Mix well.
  3. Spread the mixture into a baking dish.
  4. Top with jalapeño, green onions, red pepper, and vegan cheese.
  5. Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until heated through and cheese is melted.
  6. Garnish with sliced avocado and a drizzle of dairy-free ranch dressing before serving.

Notes

All ingredients should be vegan and dairy-free. Check product labels to ensure compliance with dietary requirements.
