Buffalo Chicken Bake

Ingredients:

  • Shredded chicken, 3 cups
  • Frozen cauliflower rice, rice or quinoa, 2 12 oz bags
  • Coconut cream, 1/4 cup
  • Sweet onion, 1
  • Green onions, 2 (plus additional for topping)
  • Dairy-free buffalo sauce, 1 bottle
  • Dairy-free ranch, 1 bottle
  • Jalapeño, 1 (plus additional for topping)
  • Garlic powder, 2 tbsp
  • Vegan cheese, 1 cup (plus additional for topping)
  • Red pepper, 1
  • Avocado, 1 (for serving)
